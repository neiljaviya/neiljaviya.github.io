About Us

We, “BAROCHIA ENTERPRISE PVT. LTD.” have introduced ourselves as a recognized manufacturer & supplier of Automotive Spare Parts. Truck Parts, Casting and forging components etc. Our preciselyengineered product range is renowned for its attributes such as high strength, accurate dimensions, easy installation and corrosion resistance. Owing to their optimum quality and durability, clients acrossdiverse automobile, and other allied sectors demandthese products from us.

We have developed a sprawling infrastructure, which is well-equipped with all the requisite tools and machinery to fabricate the wide product range. We are assisted by a dedicated team that uses latest technology and methodology to bring premium quality products for our clients. we are capable of delivering the products to the clients within predetermined time frame.

Manufacturing Process

To manufacture a world class range of high-caliber products, we have synthesized the best equation of Man and Machine at our production assembly. To efficiently carry out various processes like Selection of material, Forging/Casting, Proof Machining, Drilling, Tapping, Slotting, Milling, CNCs, VMCs, Grinding, Press Fitting and Marking that enables us to meet the stringent quality standards.

A highly skilled workforce of trained personnel make sure that every assignment goes through rigorous process of quality checks to finally deliver only the best to our clients.
